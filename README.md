Project Name: Lending_club_case_study

Description: Lending_club_case_studycompany is the largest online loan marketplace, facilitating personal loans, business loans, and financing of medical procedures. Borrowers can easily access lower interest rate loans through a fast online interface.
 

General information about the project here.
Lending_club_case_study is the largest online loan marketplace which provides loans to all sectors such as home loan, business loan, and personal loan etc. It started providing loan in 2007 so we have loan details stored from 2007 in our dataset.
What is the business probem that your project is trying to solve?

This project is about minimizing the risk of defaulters in terms of credit loss to the company by analyzing the data on historical dataset. 

What is the dataset that is being used?
Loan data set



Conclusion: check the loan amount with respect to the actual income and the home ownership of an employee before providing the loan. This analyse can be helpful in analysing the risk and avoiding credit loss to the bank.



Technologies Used
       Python, Colab, numpy, pandas, matplotlib, seaborn
